TwinPave Robotics Lab turns the ARPA-I award–winning T-GRO concept into a deployable platform: a continuously updated corridor digital twin that orchestrates semi-autonomous milling–paving–rolling–striping fleets. Our platform generates nightly overlay plans, evaluates traffic, safety, and carbon impacts, and drives robotic work-cells to execute overlays with fewer lane-closure hours and higher, more consistent quality.

What We Enable

Digital Twin–Guided Planning

2–72 hour corridor twins integrate traffic, geometry, plant production, and fleet telemetry to generate constructible, robot-ready overlay portfolios that agencies can trust and audit.

Goal: ≥ 40–50% fewer lane-closure hours.

Robotic Paving Fleets

Coordinated 3D pavers, IC rollers, and ATMA/TMA vehicles operate as synchronized robotic work-cells under the twin, reducing on-foot exposure and improving density and smoothness.

Goal: ≥ 30–50% fewer worker exposure hours.

Twin-Linked QA & Optimization

Intelligent compaction, thermal maps, and IRI snapshots stream into the twin in real time, closing the loop between robotic execution, optimization, and pay-factor quality.

Goal: ≥ 1–2 pay-factor grade improvements.

Cleaner, More Predictable Overlays

Optimized truck dispatch and rolling patterns from the twin cut idling, detours, and rework, delivering lower-carbon resurfacing and more reliable reopen times.

Goal: ≥ 10–20% CO₂e reduction.

About TwinPave Robotics Lab

TwinPave Robotics Lab is a founder-led studio translating research in digital twins, generative optimization, and construction robotics into field-ready pavement resurfacing solutions. We partner with DOTs, contractors, and OEMs to pilot twin-guided robotic overlay cells on real corridors.
